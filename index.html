Utah

Utah is a western U.S. state known for its stunning natural landscapes, including the red rock formations of Arches and Zion National Parks, the expansive Great Salt Lake, and world-class ski resorts in the Wasatch Mountains. Home to Salt Lake City, the state blends outdoor adventure with a growing tech industry, often referred to as the "Silicon Slopes." Rich in history and culture, Utah is also known for its strong pioneer heritage and vibrant communities.
